I monitor how often I get visited by the major search engine robots. GoogleBot and Yahoo Slurp regularly visit and scan thousands of pages every day. MSNBot never scans more than a couple of hundred pages in a day.

Almost all my search engine referrals are from Google. None are from MSN.
